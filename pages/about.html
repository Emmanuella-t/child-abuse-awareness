About

A calm, protective product for people who need gentleness and clarity

Safeguard is designed for children, survivors, parents, educators, and professionals. It helps people learn safely, find the next step, and practice what to do.

Our mission

Reduce harm by making safety knowledge accessible, calm, and usable. Build pathways that are easy to follow when someone feels overwhelmed, scared, or unsure.

  • Make crisis help visible, not hidden
  • Protect privacy by default
  • Teach without fear or shame

What makes this different

Many awareness sites are informational but not experiential. Safeguard is a learning product with practice and navigation tools.

  • Knowledge Playground: branching “What would you do?” scenarios
  • SafeGuide AI Companion: navigation support with strict guardrails
  • Heard: anonymous, moderated one-sentence story wall

Safe messaging principles

This site is built for vulnerable users. We follow safety and trauma-informed messaging principles:

We do

  • Use calm language and avoid graphic detail
  • Offer clear, small next steps
  • Use affirming feedback and remove shame
  • Prioritize human help in crisis
  • Keep user privacy as a core feature, not a footnote

We avoid

  • Fear-based language, threats, or guilt
  • Instructional content that teaches harm
  • Collecting personal identifying information
  • “Counseling” claims or medical/legal advice
  • Public engagement mechanics that pressure disclosure

Heard — anonymous story wall

Heard is a moderated wall where survivors can submit one sentence anonymously. Stories appear as softly animated floating cards on a dark canvas. No accounts. No public like counts.

Safety note: Do not include names, locations, or identifying details. One sentence only. Submissions are reviewed before display.

Privacy commitments

Privacy is a core feature. We design for safety and dignity.

What we do not do

  • No accounts required
  • No storing of personal identifying information
  • No recording of free-text inputs for analytics
  • No third-party ad trackers

What we do (anonymously)

  • Basic pageview analytics (privacy-first mock)
  • Local-only progress tracking for learning modules
  • Service worker caching for offline crisis resources

AI features: the browser never contains the Anthropic API key. Requests go through secure Netlify Functions with environment variables.

Important: This website provides education and navigation support. It is not a substitute for professional services.
