Reporting simulation (step by step)

This is a simulation that helps you practice what reporting can feel like. It does not send a report. It helps you plan.

What you practice

  • Staying calm and specific without overexplaining
  • Focusing on safety and observable facts
  • Knowing what you can say and what you can’t promise

What we avoid

  • Graphic descriptions
  • Investigation instructions
  • Storing any personal information
