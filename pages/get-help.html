Quick actions

  • Call your local emergency number if immediate danger is present
  • Go to a safer place if you can (a neighbor, a public place, a trusted adult)
  • If you are a professional, follow your mandated reporting protocol

If you cannot call

  • Text a trusted person: “I need help. Can you stay with me?”
  • Use Quick Exit if someone is watching
  • Write down what you remember privately, later, if safe

For children

If you feel unsafe, you deserve help. You are not in trouble. You can talk to a trusted adult like a teacher, school counselor, coach, family member you trust, or a neighbor.
