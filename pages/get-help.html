Anonymous reporting flow

You can write down what you noticed in simple, calm language. Focus on what you saw or heard, not assumptions.

  • Who is the child (first name only if needed, or “a child”)
  • What you noticed (dates, patterns, injuries, statements)
  • Where it happened (general location)
  • Why you feel concerned (brief, factual)
