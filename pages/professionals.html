Mandated reporting guidance, training, and toolkit

This page is educational and not legal advice. Follow your jurisdiction’s policy and reporting laws. If a child is in immediate danger, call emergency services.

Mandated reporter: what to do

  • Notice and document factual observations (dates, statements, visible injuries).
  • Do not investigate. Do not promise secrecy.
  • Follow your organization’s reporting workflow.
  • Contact the appropriate child protection agency or hotline for your area.
